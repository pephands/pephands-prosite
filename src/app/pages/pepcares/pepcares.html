Our Service

What We Offer

Compassionate companionship and tailored care

Companionship

Our volunteers visit patients in their homes or healthcare facilities to provide companionship and emotional support. Whether it's engaging in heartfelt conversations, going for a walk, playing games, or simply sitting quietly together, our volunteers are there to lend a caring presence.

Tailored Interactions

We understand that each individual has preferences and needs. That's why our volunteers take time to get to know each patient personally and tailor interactions. Whether it's sharing stories, reading books, listening to music, or engaging in activities, we strive to make every visit meaningful and enjoyable.

Emotional Support

Facing a serious illness can be emotionally challenging. Our volunteers offer a compassionate ear and shoulder to lean on, providing emotional support and reassurance during difficult times. We believe, by offering a safe space for expression, we can help alleviate feelings of isolation and anxiety.

Specialized Care

Pepcares Center for Cancer Affected Children

PEPCARES Center for Cancer Affected Children at the Institute of Child Health and Hospital for Children, Egmore is dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate, and cutting-edge care for children diagnosed with cancer. We are committed to improving the quality of life for our young patients and their families through state-of-the-art treatments, emotional support, and extensive research.

At the PEPCARES Center, we are at the forefront of pediatric cancer research. Our dedicated team of researchers is continually exploring new treatments and therapies to improve outcomes for our patients. We participate in clinical trials and collaborate with leading research institutions worldwide to bring the latest advancements in cancer care to our center.

At the PEPCARES Center for Cancer Affected Children, our primary focus is to provide exceptional care for children diagnosed with cancer. Located at the Institute of Child Health and Hospital for Children in Egmore, we are dedicated to offering comprehensive and compassionate services to our young patients and their families.

Holistic Palliative Support

Pillar 01

Symptom Management

Our dedicated team works round-the-clock to manage pain and alleviate severe symptoms, ensuring that every patient can focus on what matters most—spending quality time with loved ones in complete comfort.

Beyond physical care, we prioritize mental well-being. We offer expert counseling and therapeutic sessions to help patients and their families navigate the emotional challenges of chronic illness together.
