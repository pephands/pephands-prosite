As a premier pediatric oncology center in Tamil Nadu, Pepcares Center for Cancer Affected Children provides cutting-edge cancer treatment combined with holistic care. Affiliated with the renowned Institute of Child Health and Hospital for Children, Egmore, we specialize in childhood leukemia treatment, pediatric chemotherapy, and comprehensive cancer management for young patients.

Comprehensive Supportive Care for Pediatric Oncology Patients

Comprehensive Supportive Care for Pediatric Oncology Patients

Our pediatric oncology support services address all aspects of childhood cancer care, ensuring complete physical, emotional, and social support for patients and families:

Child Psychology & Family Counseling:

Licensed child psychologists conduct regular therapy sessions, while family support groups help parents cope with treatment challenges. Our trauma-informed care approach helps reduce treatment-related anxiety.

Oncology Nutrition Plans:

Certified nutritionists create customized meal plans addressing chemotherapy side effects, including specialized diets for immunosuppressed patients and nutritional rehabilitation programs.

Advanced Pain Management:

Utilizing WHO-guided pain assessment tools with multimodal analgesia techniques including PCA pumps, nerve blocks, and complementary therapies like medical massage.

Rehabilitation Medicine:

Physical therapy programs for post-surgical recovery, occupational therapy for maintaining developmental milestones, and speech therapy for treatment-affected patients.

Pediatric Oncology Education & Professional Development

Pediatric Oncology Education & Professional Development

As a regional leader in pediatric hematology-oncology education, we offer:

Fellowship Programs:

3-year DM Pediatric Oncology fellowship recognized by the Medical Council of India, with rotations in pediatric bone marrow transplantation and palliative care.

Continuing Medical Education:

Annual international pediatric oncology conferences, monthly tumor board discussions, and certification courses in pediatric palliative care.

Family Education Resources:

Multilingual treatment guides, chemotherapy education videos, and caregiver skill-building workshops available through our patient education portal.

Community Outreach & Childhood Cancer Awareness

Community Outreach & Childhood Cancer Awareness

Our community initiatives focus on early detection and support for childhood cancer:

Early Detection Campaigns:

State-wide school screening programs for common childhood cancers, community health worker training in cancer recognition, and public service announcements in regional languages.

Parent Support Networks:

Regional parent mentor program connecting new families with cancer survivors, monthly virtual support meetings, and emergency accommodation assistance.

Fundraising Initiatives:

Annual charity marathons, corporate matching gift programs, and childhood cancer awareness merchandise sales supporting our treatment subsidy fund.
