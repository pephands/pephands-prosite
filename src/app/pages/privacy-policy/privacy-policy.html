1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Pephands Foundation. At Pephands Foundation, we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of your personal information. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, maintenance, protection, and disclosure of the information we collect from you when you visit our website, use our services, participate in our events, or make a donation to our campaigns.

By accessing or using our website and services, you signify your understanding and agreement to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our website. This policy is binding on all users, donors, volunteers, and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from users in various ways in order to effectively provide our services, process donations, and keep you informed. The types of information we collect include, but are not limited to:

2.1 Information Provided Voluntarily

We collect information that you manually and voluntarily provide to us through forms, applications, and direct correspondence. This includes:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, title, and date of birth.
  • Contact Data: Email address, postal address, billing address, and telephone/mobile numbers.
  • Financial Data: When you make a donation or purchase, we collect necessary payment information. Note: All payment processing is handled securely by third-party payment gateways. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Profile Data: Your interests, preferences, feedback, and survey responses related to our NGO activities.
  • Communication Data: Records and copies of your correspondence (including email addresses) if you contact us.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

As you navigate through and interact with our website, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including:

  • Technical Data: Internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, products, and services, including page response times, download errors, length of visits to certain pages, page interaction information (such as scrolling, clicks, and mouse-overs), and methods used to browse away from the page.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used to fulfill our mission as an NGO and to provide you with the best possible experience. We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Process Donations and Contributions: To manage payments, issue tax-deductible receipts, and maintain a record of your contributions for legal and accounting purposes.
  • To Communicate With You: To send you newsletters, updates about our campaigns, events, and initiatives. This includes transactional emails (e.g., donation confirmations) and promotional emails (which you can opt out of at any time).
  • To Personalize User Experience: To understand how our users as a group use the services and resources provided on our website and to tailor our content to better suit your preferences.
  • To Improve Our Website: To analyze trends, track user interactions, troubleshoot technical issues, and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns and website functionality.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To enforce our policies, comply with local and international laws, respond to lawful requests from public authorities, and protect the rights, property, or safety of Pephands Foundation, our users, or others.
  • To Manage Volunteers and Partnerships: To process applications for volunteering, CSR partnerships, and employment opportunities.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and strictly prohibit the sale, trading, or renting of your personal identification information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information under the following specific circumstances:

  • Trusted Service Providers: We may share information with trusted partners and third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our operations, processing payments, or servicing you, provided that these parties agree to keep this information strictly confidential and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency), or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
  • Business Transfers: In the highly unlikely event of a reorganization, merger, or asset sale of the Foundation, user information may be transferred as a business asset, subject to the pre-existing Privacy Policy remaining in force.

5. Security Measures and Data Retention

We are deeply committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement appropriate and robust physical, electronic, and managerial security measures to protect users' personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Specific measures include:

  • Encryption: Utilizing Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to ensure that all data transmitted between your web browser and our servers is encrypted and secure.
  • Access Controls: Restricting access to personal information only to authorized employees, volunteers, and contractors who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Security Audits: Conducting regular vulnerability scanning and security audits to ensure our digital infrastructure remains impregnable to modern threats.

Data Retention: We will only retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Cookie Policy and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses "cookies" and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and provide personalized content. A cookie is a small text file placed on your hard drive by a web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are required for the operation of our website (e.g., logging into secure areas or processing shopping cart items).
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our website. This helps us improve the way our website works.
  • Functionality Cookies: These are used to recognize you when you return to our website, allowing us to personalize our content for you and remember your preferences.

Managing Cookies: Users have the option to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features and functionality of the website, resulting in a suboptimal browsing experience.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data stored by us.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, particularly for direct marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy
