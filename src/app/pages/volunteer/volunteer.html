Volunteering

Building Hope, Creating Change

Welcome to Pephands, where we believe in the power of collective action to create positive change. Our mission is to uplift communities and make a meaningful impact in the lives of those in need. We are a non-profit organization based in Chennai, with a vision to expand our reach across India.

Pephands engages with volunteers primarily through partnerships with volunteer programs across India. If you hold an undergraduate degree, possess significant work experience, and are interested in volunteering opportunities nationwide, you may be eligible to join Pephands. Our organization collaborates with various volunteer programs to recruit and place volunteers in assignments that support our mission. We do not directly recruit volunteers; instead, we encourage individuals to explore opportunities through these programs.

At Pephands, our volunteers not only assist in implementing our programs but also play a vital role in advancing the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By joining us as a volunteer, you become an agent of positive change.
