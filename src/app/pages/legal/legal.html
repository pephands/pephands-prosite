Our Commitment to Transparency

At Pephands Foundation, we are committed to operating with the highest level of integrity, ethical standards, and legal compliance. As a registered non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India, we ensure that every donation is accounted for and every campaign is conducted within the boundaries of the law.

1. Corporate & Regulatory Compliance

Pephands Foundation is legally registered as a charitable trust/society under the laws of the Government of India. Our operations strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth by regulatory authorities for non-profit entities.

  • Financial Auditing: Our financial records undergo rigorous annual audits by independent, certified chartered accountants to ensure full transparency.
  • Fund Utilization: We are legally bound to utilize all received funds entirely for charitable objectives, including education, healthcare, hunger relief, and community development.
