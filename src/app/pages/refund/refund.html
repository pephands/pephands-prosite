1. Donations and Contributions

At Pephands Foundation, we are profoundly grateful for the generosity of our donors. Your financial support directly enables us to carry out our vital charitable activities, fund campaigns, and make a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve.

Because these contributions are immediately allocated to active projects and relief efforts, all donations and contributions made to Pephands Foundation are considered final and non-refundable. Once a transaction is successfully processed, the funds are treated as an irrevocable gift to the Foundation.

We deeply appreciate your commitment to our cause and encourage you to review all donation amounts and details carefully prior to authorizing any payment.

2. Exceptional Circumstances for Refunds

While refunds are generally not offered under standard conditions, we recognize that technical errors and exceptional circumstances can occur. In such specific scenarios, a refund may be warranted and authorized by our financial team. These circumstances strictly include:

  • Transaction Errors: In the event of a verifiable transaction error—such as duplicate charges, accidental overbilling due to website glitches, or an erroneous extra zero inputted during payment—please contact us immediately. We will thoroughly investigate the digital footprint of the transaction and take necessary steps to rectify it by refunding the excess amount.
  • Unauthorized Charges: If you believe there has been a completely unauthorized, fraudulent charge to your credit card or bank account originating from our platform, please notify us and your financial institution promptly. We will conduct a thorough internal investigation, halt the use of those specific funds, and work cooperatively with you and your bank to resolve the issue and process a refund if fraud is confirmed.

3. Refund Request Process

If your situation falls under the exceptional circumstances outlined above, you must submit a formal refund request within 7 days of the original transaction date. Requests made outside of this window will not be eligible for review.

To request a refund, you must provide:

  • Your full name and contact information.
  • The date and exact amount of the transaction.
  • The payment method used (do not send full credit card numbers).
  • The transaction ID or receipt number provided at the time of donation.
  • A clear explanation of the error or reason for the refund request.

Approved refunds will be processed and credited back to the original method of payment within 10-15 business days, depending on your bank's processing times.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, require clarification about our refund policy, or need immediate assistance with a transaction error, please do not hesitate to reach out. Our dedicated support team is here to assist you and ensure a positive, transparent experience with Pephands Foundation.
