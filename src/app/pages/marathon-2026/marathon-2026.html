About the Event

Run For Life Marathon 2026

More than a race — it became a monumental movement against drug addiction.

The Run For Life Marathon in Chennai 2026, organised by the Pephands Foundation, brought together thousands of citizens, students, athletes, and community leaders who stood united against the catastrophic dangers of drug addiction.

The 2026 edition took place on Sunday, June 14, 2026, at Olcott School Road, Besant Nagar, Chennai. With over 1500+ expected runners turning up for the 3K and 5K runs, the event was a spectacular display of fitness, solidarity, and a shared commitment to a drug-free future.

Every stride taken on the scenic route of Besant Nagar was a powerful statement of resilience. The marathon successfully bridged the gap between health advocacy and community empowerment, demonstrating to the youth that true strength lies in making positive, life-affirming choices rather than succumbing to peer pressure and substance abuse.

Why We Ran

A Movement of Purpose

Reflecting on the vision, community spirit, and legacy behind the Run For Life Marathon.

01
The Vision

A Stride Against Addiction

The Run For Life Marathon, the Best Marathon in Chennai, was more than a race; it was a unified stance against addiction in our communities. In a world where youth are increasingly vulnerable to peer pressure, this event created a vibrant, supportive space that celebrated health, solidarity, and hope. By prioritising purpose over financial incentives, we gathered everyday champions to share stories of strength and recovery. The funds and awareness generated continue to empower the Pephands Foundation to conduct life-saving school workshops and support systems, proving that true change happens when our community moves forward together.

02
The Cause

Purpose Over Prizes

Whether participants raced the 5K or joined the 3K fun run, their participation in the Best Social Cause Marathon in Chennai went far beyond the finish line. We ran with heart rather than for cash prizes, uniting students, professionals, and local leaders to raise awareness against substance abuse. Every step taken along Olcott School Road empowered the Pephands Foundation, making every runner an active ambassador for a healthier, brighter future.
