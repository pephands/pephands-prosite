Super Mom Initiative

Milk of Kindness, Mothers Helping Premature Babies Thrive

Nurturing tiny lives through a mother’s selfless gift of love and nourishment.

Since 2023

Launched in 2023, our Super Mom Initiative collects breast milk from generous mothers to support premature and critically ill newborns.

140,048 ml

To date, 140,048 ml of milk has been collected and donated to the Institute of Child Health (ICH) Neonatal Intensive Care Unit, Egmore, Chennai.

1,400+

Through this life-saving act of compassion, over 1,400 babies have benefited, receiving vital nutrition during their most vulnerable stage.
