Breastfeeding Overview

Ensuring a Healthy Start for Every Child

Global Health Priority

Breastfeeding is crucial for child health, but less than half of infants under 6 months are exclusively breastfed.

Safety & Protection

Breast Milk is a safe, clean, and protective food for infants, providing energy and nutrients for the first months, and providing up to half or more during the second half and one-third during the second year.

Long-term Benefits

Breastfeeding improves children's intelligence, reduces obesity, diabetes, and reduces breast and ovarian cancer risk.
