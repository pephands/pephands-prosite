Global Campaign

World Breastfeeding Week

August 1st to 7th, Every Year

Global Awareness

Breastfeeding Week, a global campaign from August 1-7, raises awareness about breastfeeding's importance and advocates for support and protection. Founded by WABA in 1992, it's celebrated in 120 countries.

Health & Education

Breastfeeding Week promotes breastfeeding as a vital maternal and child health aspect, educating and empowering mothers and families about its benefits, including optimal nutrition, immune system boost, and strong bond.

Policy & Support

Breastfeeding Week addresses challenges and barriers mothers face in breastfeeding, advocating for supportive policies, maternity leave, workplace accommodations, and skilled lactation support.
